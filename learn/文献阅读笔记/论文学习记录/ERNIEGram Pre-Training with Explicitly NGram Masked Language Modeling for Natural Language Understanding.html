# ERNIE-Gram: Pre-Training with Explicitly N-Gram Masked Language Modeling for Natural Language Understanding

作者：百度团队

文献类型：arXiv

关键词：自然语言处理

# Abstract

英 汉
Coarse-grained linguistic information, such as named entities or phrases, facilitates adequately representation learning in pre-training. 粗粒度的语言信息，例如命名实体或短语，有助于在预训练中充分地进行表征学习。
Previous works mainly focus on extending the objective of BERT’s Masked Language Modeling (MLM) from masking individual tokens to contiguous sequences of n tokens. 以前的工作主要集中在将 BERT 的掩码语言建模 (MLM) 的目标从掩码单个token扩展到 n 个token的连续序列。
We argue that such contiguously masking method neglects to model the intra-dependencies and inter-relation of coarse-grained linguistic information. 我们认为，这种连续屏蔽方法忽略了对粗粒度语言信息的内部依赖性和相互关系进行建模。
As an alternative, we propose ERNIE-Gram, an explicitly n-gram masking method to enhance the integration of coarse-grained information into pre-training. 作为替代方案，我们提出了 ERNIE-Gram，这是一种明确的 n-gram 掩码方法，用于增强粗粒度信息与预训练的集成。
In ERNIE-Gram,n-grams are masked and predicted directly using explicit n-gram identities rather than contiguous sequences of n tokens. 在ERNIE-Gram中，n-grams被屏蔽，并直接使用明确的n-gram身份而不是n个tokens的连续序列来进行预测。
Furthermore,ERNIE-Gram employs a generator model to sample plausible n-gram identities as optional n-gram masks and predict them in both coarse grained and fifine-grained manners to enable comprehensive n-gram prediction and relation modeling. 此外，ERNIE-Gram 使用生成器模型对合理的 n-gram 身份进行采样作为可选的 n-gram 掩码，并以粗粒度和细粒度的方式预测它们，以实现全面的 n-gram 预测和关系建模。
We pre-train ERNIE-Gram on English and Chinese text corpora and fifinetune on 19 downstream tasks. 我们在英文和中文文本语料库上预训练 ERNIE-Gram，并在 19 个下游任务上进行微调。
Experimental results show that ERNIE-Gram outperforms previous pre-training models like XLNet and RoBERTa by a large margin, and achieves comparable results with state-of-the-art methods. 实验结果表明，ERNIE-Gram 在很大程度上优于先前的预训练模型，如 XLNet 和 RoBERTa，并取得了与最先进方法相当的结果。

# 1 Introduction

英 汉
Pre-trained on large-scaled text corpora and fifinetuned on downstream tasks, self-supervised representation models have achieved remarkable improvements in natural language understanding (NLU). 在大规模文本语料库上进行预训练并在下游任务上进行微调，自监督表示模型在自然语言理解 (NLU) 方面取得了显着的进步。
As one of the most prominent pre-trained models, BERT employs masked language modeling (MLM) to learn representations by masking individual tokens and predicting them based on their bidirectional context. 作为最突出的预训练模型之一，BERT 采用掩码语言建模 (MLM) 来通过掩码单个token并根据其双向上下文预测它们来学习表示。
However, BERT’s MLM focuses on the represen tations of fifine-grained text units (e.g. words or subwords in English and characters in Chinese),rarely considering the coarse-grained linguistic information (e.g. named entities or phrases in English and words in Chinese) thus incurring inadequate representation learning. 然而，BERT 的 MLM 侧重于细粒度文本单元（例如英文单词或子词和中文字符）的表示，很少考虑粗粒度的语言信息（例如英文命名实体或短语和中文单词），因此导致表示学习不足。
Many efforts have been devoted to integrate coarse-grained semantic information by independently masking and predicting contiguous sequences of n tokens, namely n-grams, such as named entities, phrases, whole words and text spans. 许多人致力于通过独立屏蔽和预测n个 token的连续序列，即n-grams，如命名实体、短语、整个单词和文本跨度来整合粗粒度的语义信息。
We argue that such contiguously masking strategies are less effective and reliable since the prediction of tokens in masked n-grams are independent of each other, which neglects the intra dependencies of n-grams. 我们认为，这种连续掩蔽的策略不太有效和可靠，因为被掩蔽的n-grams中的tokens的预测是相互独立的，这就忽略了n-grams的内部依赖关系。
